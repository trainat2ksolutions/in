Meet the Founder

Tukaram (2K)

Founder & CEO of 2K Solutions

Tukaram (2K), the visionary behind **2K Solutions**, is a passionate educator and tech enthusiast who has dedicated his career to empowering individuals with the skills needed to thrive in the tech industry.

Born on 15 June 2000 in the small village of Jainapur, Tukaram pursued his Engineering degree and then worked as a software developer for 1.5 years in top-tier software development companies. During this time, he gained valuable insights into the tech industry and honed his programming and problem-solving skills.

Driven by a passion for teaching, Tukaram transitioned to become a trainer at an international training company, where he worked for nearly two years. This experience allowed him to share his knowledge with aspiring tech professionals and help them build the skills necessary to excel in the fast-paced world of software development.

With the goal of providing comprehensive training, mentorship, and placement support, Tukaram founded **2K Solutions**. His mission is to bridge the gap between education and employment by offering students the tools and resources they need to succeed in their careers.

Under his leadership, **2K Solutions** has grown into a leading educational institute, offering top-tier software training and job placement assistance to individuals seeking to advance their careers in the tech industry.
